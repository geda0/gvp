What did you build at IBM Apptio?
At IBM (Apptio) I worked on Cloudability — the cloud financial management product. My focus was platform architecture for subscription and identity flows: defining service boundaries, making the ingestion pipelines operable, and tightening the loop between billing events and customer-facing reporting. A lot of it was data-intensive SaaS work where correctness and throughput matter equally.
Would that translate to a staff platform role at a Series C startup?
Yes — the patterns transfer cleanly. Series C is usually where boundary definition starts paying off: you've outgrown the monolith but can't yet afford a fragmented service mesh. My JumpCloud and HP work was closer to that scale, so I've seen the failure modes both directions.
